Jennet Clouston is a marginal character from Robert Louis Stevenson's novel "Kidnapped". The main character David Balfour meets the "stout, dark, sour-looking woman" on his way to the House of Shaws in Southern Scotland, the castle of his anchestors. Asked for the way to the mansion, Jennet casts one of the best curses in literature on the House of Shaws and it's current landlord, Ebenezer Balfour.

A word about the Web Speech Api

The Web Speech API is a relatively new HTML5 standard. It will enable browsers to render text to audio and audio to text.

The API is not implemented yet on most browsers and even if it is, then it depends also on your system. Google/Chrome and Apple/Safari claim full support, however testing shows that there are still a number of issues and inconsistencies in regards to sound buffering and performance.

So chances are that the app actually doesn't work on your device.
